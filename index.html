Nathan Morse

Hi, so I'm Nathan! I'm graduating from Wheaton College in Massachusetts in May as a Computer Science Major and Studio Art Minor. The pictures on my website are all my own, many of which were taken while in New Zealand. For the Spring semester of my Junior year at Wheaton, I studied abroad in New Zealand at the University of Auckland, and I found that I learned a heck of a lot more than just things about computer science or art. I could go on and on about my four and a half journey but then this page would be far too long!

Other than being a happy traveler, I am very hands on. I love to learn and appreciate how things work from the base up. I enjoy fixing just about anything, especially if it involves learning something new in the process. Admittedly, I am fairly stubborn about solving problems and I don't give up until I achieve my goal. I also love to make things from scratch, whether it be some kind of word parser or musical instrument.

You are welcomed to check out some of my artwork on this page, but also feel free to visit my LinkedIn and GitHub, which can also be found at the foot of the page. A pdf copy of my current resume is below.
